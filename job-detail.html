Job description

Tracks, analyses, and reports on key metrics regarding the business’s online audience through data analysis from which he is able to build and continuously enhance the business’s search rankings on major search engines

Conduct competitive site analysis

Perform SEO audits and analyses

Work alongside multiple different stakeholders to achieve Shopee marketing objectives through SEO Marketing end to end initiatives

Actively seeking for improvement areas by closely monitoring the Shopee and its products' SEO performances (clicks, ranking, market share, etc.) and staying alert for any changes in the Shopee website that may harm SEO

Develop plans for sustainable yet cost-efficient Shopee SEO optimisation that covers operational aspects

Implement successful improvement and/or optimisation of on-page SEO projects such as technology adoptions, feature enablement, experiments, and issue management

Identifying consolidation opportunities of Shopee SEO improvements from all products in Shopee

Requirment

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science/Engineering or related background

Minimum 4 years of experience in managing and/or developing SEO technical improvement as well as developing SEO content preferably at a large-scale e-commerce, OTA, or SEO agency

A deep understanding of technical SEO and its relation to ranking algorithms such as crawl, index, metadata, Google Search Algorithm and Ranking System, and other on-page factors is a must.

Experienced with SEO relevant tools (e.g. Ahrefs, ScreamingFrog, Similarweb etc.) and web analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics, Google Search Console, Google Data Studio)

Experience with SEO reporting
